Life in Bergen

This is my first time living abroad. There are some places I spend lots of my time in Bergen

School

This is university of Bergen, the building of Humanities faculty. Every morning I go to school. It has very nice classrooms, libraries, cafeterias. Lectures are also very well organized. I spend most of my daytime here taking courses.

Light rail

To go to city center or to school, I have to take the light rail. It takes around 30 minutes from Fantoft to city center. The light rail has big windows so I can see beautiful scenery of city Bergen. When I passing through with hearing music, I always feel like my heart is full of emotion.

Mountain

It seems like Norwegian loves to hike. Bergen has 7 mountains, and every weekend people here go to hike. Some mountains are quite hard to hike, but whenever I hike mountain, I become energetic and feel healthy.
